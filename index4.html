Moment of Music

This collection captures the euphoric energy of MALIQ & D’Essentials through primary colors and dramatic lens flare techniques. My main focus was freezing the stage dynamics and intimate interactions between the musicians into a vibrant visual harmony.

The use of starburst effects and light textures is designed to recreate the concert's intense atmosphere. Each frame is more than just documentation; it is an effort to record the "soul" of every beat radiating from the stage.

Capturing the essence of time and beauty in every moment. Delivering professional documentation with high artistic standards.
