About

Me

Born in 2000, Farhanhers made a name for himself in the art world with his vibrant & evocative photography. His work is highly stylized yet infused with a sense of warmth, capturing the essence of city life from a deeply personal & aesthetic perspective.

His photographs allow viewers to connect with the stories of others, while the skill & character of his photographs enhance his visual storytelling.

Through the images, Farhan was able to go several steps beyond his reality. He was transported to a mental realm he rarely experienced free from all thoughts.

There he could communicate without words but still say a lot. The images took him to places he would never have visited, and they held the answers to his curiosity.
